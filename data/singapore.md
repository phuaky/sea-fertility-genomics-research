# Singapore — IVF Clinic Directory

> **Government Registry:** No standalone list published. Licensed AR centres verifiable via:
> - [HealthHub Directory](https://eservices.healthhub.sg/healthhubng/public/services/directory) (JS app)
> - [HCSA AR Service page](https://www.hcsa.gov.sg/outpatient-services/assisted-reproduction-service/)
> - [FertilitySupport.sg clinic list](https://fertilitysupport.sg/list-of-clinics-groups/) (~13 confirmed licensed centres)
>
> **Regulatory framework:** HCSA 2020 + ARS Regulations 2023. PGT-A still in pilot trial (inconclusive as of Jan 2024).
> **Only MOH-approved PGT lab:** NUH Pre-implantation Genetic Diagnosis Centre (PGDC).
